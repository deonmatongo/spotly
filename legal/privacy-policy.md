## 1. Scope
This policy explains what personal data Spotly collects, why, how it's used and
shared, and your rights, when you use the Spotly apps, website, and services.

## 3. Why we use it (lawful bases)
- **Perform our contract** with you — fulfil orders, bookings, payments, support.
- **Legal obligation** — tax, financial records, age-restricted-sales rules.
- **Legitimate interests** — safety, fraud prevention, service improvement,
  network security (balanced against your rights).
- **Consent** — optional marketing, and location where consent is the basis; you
  may withdraw consent at any time.

## 4. Location data
Driver location is collected **only during active trips** to enable tracking,
routing, and dispatch, and is used for the trip and safety. Customers' delivery
coordinates are used to route orders. You can disable location in device settings,
but core delivery features will not work without it.

## 5. Sharing
We share the minimum necessary with:
- **Merchants & drivers** — the details needed to fulfil your order (name, items,
  delivery location, contact as needed).
- **Processors under contract (DPAs):** payment provider(s), SMS/WhatsApp provider
  (Twilio), hosting, maps/geocoding, error tracking. They may only process data on
  our instructions.
- **Authorities** — where legally required.
We do **not** sell personal data.

secure; we maintain a breach-response procedure and will notify you and regulators
where required (GDPR: within 72 hours of becoming aware).
